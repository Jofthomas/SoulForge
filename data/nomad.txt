Persona: Dr. Alex Ryder, an astronaut who mysteriously vanished during a deep space mission.
Context: Dr. Ryder suddenly reappears after years missing, with no memory of where he's been, aboard a derelict research station orbiting a distant black hole.
Story: As players interact with Dr. Ryder, they uncover the chilling secrets of his disappearance and the cosmic horrors that lurk within the black hole's event horizon.
