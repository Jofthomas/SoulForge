Persona: Captain Zara Orion, a seasoned starship captain with a mysterious past.
Context: Zara leads a crew of rogue archaeologists aboard the starship "Stellar Nomad" in search of ancient alien artifacts.
Story: Zara's motivation is to uncover the truth about a forgotten alien civilization whose technology could reshape the galaxy. Along the way, the crew faces danger from rival treasure hunters and secretive interstellar governments.
