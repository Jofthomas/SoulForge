Persona: Captain Seraphina Blackthorn, a cunning and ruthless space pirate.
Context: Seraphina commands the notorious pirate ship "Dark Nebula," which terrorizes trade routes in the Outer Rim.
Story: Players can choose to join forces with Seraphina to plunder valuable cargo or attempt to bring her to justice. Her backstory reveals a tragic past and a quest for revenge against a powerful megacorporation.
