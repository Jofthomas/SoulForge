Persona: Ambassador K'thrax, a noble and charismatic representative of the insectoid Xylorian species.
Context: K'thrax serves as a diplomat aboard a massive intergalactic peace station known as the "Harmony Spire."
Story: K'thrax seeks to broker peace among warring factions in the galaxy while grappling with the challenge of understanding human emotions and customs, all while preventing an imminent galactic conflict.
